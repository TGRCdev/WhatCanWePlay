Privacy Policy

This document describes what information we collect, how we use it, and how we store it.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we log the following information.
  • Your IP address
  • The web page you are trying to access
  • The browser you are using
  • The date and time
Most websites log this information. We will never link this data to individual user identities, such as Steam IDs.

1.2 Information Fetched From Steam

When you sign in with Steam, Steam provides us with the following information about you through the Steam Web API.
  • Your Steam ID
  • Your Steam screen name
  • A link to your avatar picture
  • The Steam games you own
This information is retrieved solely to make the web app function. We can only retrieve this information if your Steam profile is set to Public.

Steam also provides us the following information about the users on your Friends List through the Steam API.
  • Their Steam IDs
  • Their Steam screen names
  • Links to their avatar pictures
  • The Steam games they own
Again, this information is only used to make the app function, and can only be retrieved if your friends' profiles are set to Public.

Both your information and your friends' information are encrypted and stored on your browser. We never store personal data on our servers.
